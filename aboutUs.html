About Us Glad to be here!

Magzhub started with an idea of developing a platform from where readers can enjoy their favourite magazines & books on the go, whenever wherever they want, along with a cost effective platform for publishers to serve their readers. Magzhub presents a unique revenue-share based business model to publishers that make it easier for them to offer digital editions of their magazines globally with NO upfront cost. Magzhub is tested in front of global technology & management leaders. Magzhub is your very own personal library which you can carry right in your pocket and can access directly from Magzhub.com which has hundreds of magazines & books.

Go digital today with Magzhub and enjoy your favourite magazines & books anytime, anywhere.
